We are more than just a Taxi company

KFUPM Ride is a web-application that helps students from KFUPM to schedule their rides from the university to places that students usually go to and vise-versa.
Drivers will be KFUPM students who want to make a profit. Also, it enables students to share rides to reduce the cost on them.
